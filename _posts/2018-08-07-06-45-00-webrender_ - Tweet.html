Smart trick:
If you are replacing the normal focus outline with a different focus effect, don't use `outline: none`, use `outline-color: transparent`, to support accessible color schemes.

(For the same reason, always keep borders on your buttons, even if they are transparent.) https://t.co/YwMhCfdQUf
